About AiDiA

Built on the belief that no one should feel alone

Loneliness is one of the most serious challenges facing people today — from elderly residents in care facilities, to individuals living alone, to teenagers who feel disconnected from the world around them.

AiDiA was created to address that gap. Combining the comfort of a soft, tactile companion with advanced conversational AI, AiDiA offers something genuinely new: a presence that listens without judgement, engages without tiring, and cares without condition.
